The Process

How Financial Assistance Works

The process is straightforward and respectful of your time. Here is what to expect from start to finish.

01

Request a Review

Reach out to us to begin a brief eligibility review. This is an initial step to determine whether you may qualify — no commitments required.

02

Eligibility Determination

Your request is reviewed according to our program guidelines. Approval and assistance amounts are not guaranteed and vary by individual circumstance.

03

Assistance Decision

If approved, the details of your assistance — including the amount and permitted use — will be communicated to you clearly and in advance before anything is finalized.

Eligibility

Who Can Apply

Financial assistance is available to individuals who meet our program criteria. Each case is reviewed individually.

Eligibility is assessed through a brief review and may differ based on your specific situation. Meeting certain criteria does not guarantee approval, and participating in public programs or other services does not affect your standing with us.

All applications are reviewed fairly and independently. We encourage anyone who believes they may qualify to reach out and request a review — there is no cost or obligation to do so.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What can the financial assistance be used for? +
Permitted use of assistance depends on the program guidelines applicable to your case. If approved, this will be explained to you clearly before assistance is provided — nothing will come as a surprise.
Is assistance tied to any provider or service? +
No. All assistance decisions are made independently. We do not provide assistance in exchange for referrals, partnerships, or participation with any organization or individual.
Is approval guaranteed? +
No. Approval and assistance amounts vary by individual. We review each application according to our guidelines and will communicate our decision clearly.
How do I confirm I'm really speaking with Sahan Foundation? +
You can always hang up and call us back directly using the phone number listed on this website. We will also never ask for unsafe forms of payment — that is always a red flag.
